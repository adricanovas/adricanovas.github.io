About

I'm a PhD student at the University of Murcia working on Knowledge Acquisition through Artificial Intelligence Techniques for Sustainable Environments. My research applies machine learning and remote sensing to environmental monitoring challenges, with a current focus on water quality assessment in coastal lagoon ecosystems.

I enjoy building tools that bridge data science and real-world problems — from satellite imagery pipelines to automation bots. Outside research, I work on software projects spanning computer vision, data engineering, and web development.
